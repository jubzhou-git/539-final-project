You are a sunflower!

Optimistic | Energetic | Loyal | Bright

You have an infectious enthusiasm that draws people in. You always turn toward the good in situations and in people, and your consistency makes you one of the most dependable friends anyone could ask for.

Fun fact: Sunflowers are not actually a single flower — each head is made up of up to 2,000 tiny individual flowers called florets!

Floriography: Sunflowers symbolize adoration, loyalty, and longevity. Their habit of turning to face the sun gave them the meaning of devoted love and unwavering faith.

Origin: Native to North America
Best conditions: Full sun, well-drained soil, warm climates
