You are a sunflower!

Optimistic | Energetic | Loyal | Bright

You have a warmth that draws people in instantly. Radiant and endlessly supportive, you are the friend who shows up with a big hug and an open heart when things get tough. Your energy is contagious, and you have a natural gift for making others feel seen, included, and celebrated. Like a sunflower constantly turning toward the light, you focus on the good in life and reflect that joy back onto everyone lucky enough to be in your orbit.

Fun fact: Sunflowers are not actually a single flower — each head is made up of up to 2,000 tiny individual flowers called florets.

Floriography: Sunflowers symbolize adoration, loyalty, and longevity. Their habit of turning to face the sun gave them the meaning of devoted love and unwavering faith.

Origin: Native to North America

Best conditions: Full sun, well-drained soil, warm climates
