You are a rose!

Passionate | Romantic | Expressive | Resilient

You feel everything deeply and aren't afraid to show it. You have strong opinions, a big heart, and a natural elegance. Like a rose, you have both softness and strength — and you're never boring.

Fun fact: Roses are one of the oldest cultivated flowers — fossilized rose specimens have been found that are 35 million years old!

Floriography: Roses are perhaps the richest flower in symbolic meaning — red roses represent deep love and passion, pink convey admiration and gratitude, white symbolize purity, and yellow signal friendship and joy. Color matters!

Origin: Native to Asia, with some species from Europe, North America, and Africa
Best conditions: Full sun, fertile and well-drained soil, regular watering
