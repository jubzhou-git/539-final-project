You are a rose!

Passionate | Romantic | Expressive | Resilient

You experience the world with your whole heart. Passionate, fiercely devoted, and deeply romantic, you don't do anything halfway. You value authentic, intimate connection and aren't afraid to dive into the intense depths of human emotion. People are captivated by your elegance, your expressive nature, and your unyielding loyalty. Like a rose, you possess both breathtaking beauty and the necessary strength to fiercely protect what matters most to you.

Fun fact: Roses are one of the oldest cultivated flowers. Some rose specimens have been found to be 35 million years old.

Floriography: Red roses represent deep love and passion, pink roses convey admiration and gratitude, and yellow roses signal friendship and joy.

Origin: Native to Asia, with some species from Europe, North America, and Africa

Best conditions: Full sun, fertile and well-drained soil, regular watering
