You are a tulip!

Hopeful | Graceful | Adaptable | Refreshing

You are full of new beginnings. You bring a sense of optimism and renewal to the spaces you're in, and you handle change with more grace than most. People are uplifted just by being around your energy.

Fun fact: During "Tulip Mania" in 17th-century Holland, tulip bulbs became so valuable that they were used as currency — and a single bulb could cost more than a house!

Floriography: Tulips broadly symbolize perfect love and declaration of affection. Red tulips in particular are a bold declaration of love, while purple tulips represent royalty, and white tulips are used to ask for forgiveness or convey worthiness.

Origin: Native to Central Asia, popularized in the Netherlands
Best conditions: Cool climates, full to partial sun, well-drained and fertile soil
