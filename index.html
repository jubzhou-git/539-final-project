🌸 The Flower Personality Quiz 🌸

Discover What Flower You Are

For my final project, I created a flower personality quiz. The questions connect personality traits with plant traits: for example, people who are adaptable in new environments would resemble a flower that is able to grow in many different climates. There are a total of 6 results: daisy, lavender, orchid, rose, sunflower, and tulip.
