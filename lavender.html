You are a lavender!

Thoughtful | Peaceful | Reflective | Balanced

You appreciate quiet moments and peaceful environments. You tend to think things through before acting and value balance in your life. Your presence has a calming effect on the people around you.

Fun fact: Lavender has been used for over 2,500 years — ancient Egyptians used it in the mummification process, and Romans added it to their baths!

Floriography: Lavender symbolizes serenity, grace, and devotion. It has long been associated with silence and calm, and was historically given as a token of affection and a wish for the recipient's tranquility.

Origin: Native to the Mediterranean region
Best conditions: Full sun, dry and well-drained soil, warm and rocky terrain
