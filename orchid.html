You are an orchid!

Rare | Perceptive | Independent | Refined

You are a complex person and have a unique presence. Independent and highly thoughtful, you don't feel the need to blend in with the crowd or over-explain yourself. You prefer deep, meaningful interactions and curated spaces that allow you to think, create, and exist purely on your own terms. Your authenticity and charisma is what makes you thrive.

Fun fact: Orchids are one of the largest plant families on Earth, with over 25,000 species. They can be found on every continent except Antarctica.

Floriography: Orchids symbolize rare beauty, luxury, and strength. In ancient Greece they were associated with virility and power, while in Victorian England they became a symbol of refined taste and exotic elegance.

Origin: Found worldwide, with high diversity in tropical regions

Best conditions: Indirect light, high humidity, good airflow, minimal watering
