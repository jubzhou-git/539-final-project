You are a daisy!

Easygoing | Cheerful | Genuine | Grounded

Even when you're not the center of attention, you have a joyful energy about you that makes everyday moments for others feel special. People love you for your realness and your easy, warm presence. You are adaptable and you also feel excited by spontaneous situations. Like a daisy pushing through a crack in the sidewalk, your optimism is tenacious even in difficult situations.

Fun fact: The name "daisy" comes from the Old English phrase "day's eye" because the flower opens at dawn and closes at dusk.

Floriography: Daisies symbolize innocence, purity, and new beginnings. They are also associated with true love and the old tradition of plucking petals to the chant of "they love me, they love me not."

Origin: Native to Europe and western Asia

Best conditions: Moderate sun, well-drained soil, cool to mild temperatures and climates
